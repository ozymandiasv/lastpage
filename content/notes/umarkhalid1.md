The Supreme Court has clarified that prolonged incarceration is a definitive ground for bail, which is highly relevant to Umar Khalid's case. If an accusation cannot be proven or brought to trial after 3-4 years, it reflects either a false charge or a broken system. In either scenario, the accused is entitled to bail.
