The extremely strict bail rules under laws like UAPA and PMLA have introduced an unacceptable system of indefinite jail time for people who haven't even been proven guilty. This directly breaks the constitutional rights to life, personal freedom, and a fair trial under Articles 21 and 22. It completely flips the basic rule of justice that everyone is innocent until proven guilty. Just as it is trying to do with UAPA, the Supreme Court must heavily intervene to curb the misuse of the PMLA.
