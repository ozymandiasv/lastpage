The genius of Attack on Titan is how quietly it swaps genres on you. What begins as survival horror behind a wall becomes a story about propaganda, generational trauma, and the impossibility of a clean ending to any war. Few finales have been as divisive, and few have earned the right to be that uncomfortable.

## Verdict

Essential. Not because it is perfect, but because it refuses to let you leave with easy answers.
