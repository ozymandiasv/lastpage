---
title: "Surviving Today"
type: "Verse"
date: "2026-03-05"
published: true
---

My biggest problem is surviving today.
I have been here longer than I knew you,
longer than most things I thought would last,
and still I wake with the weight already there.
Not grief. Something without a name.
The kind that sits in your chest like furniture
nobody ordered but nobody moves,
patient in the way only permanence can be.

The ache makes no sound, asks for nothing,
just sits where I used to be lighter,
where something once believed tomorrow meant something,
where I once looked up without thinking twice.
I still wake. Still climb. Still stay.
But the chest knows what the mind won't say.
That this is the place where hope used to be found,
before it learned the truth and went to ground.
